Explore the Art Culture of The Philippine Islands.

One of the best cultural art of the Philippines is

Bayanihan (1959)

Fernando Amorsolo

Bayanihan (1959), the process of a village moving a house in unison, is both the title and subject matter of Amorsolo's painting, and is a typical example of the "conservative realist" strain of painting that dominated the Philippine art scene in the period directly following independence from the United States in 1946.

About us

What is Jeepney Journey?

The Jeepney Journey is here for promoting cultural arts and crafts of the Philippine Islands, with a focus on the cultural art of the Infamous artists of the islands. Emphasizing the artists that work their way to the top, giving Philippines the recognition it needs and bring pride to the motherland.

Cultural Arts

Philippines is consists of 7,641 islands where every islands is full of creativity and smart minds full of imagination. Jeepney Journey is here to assist you on a one of a kind journey to the islands presenting their arts, cultures, and traditions.

Cultural Activities

There's a lot of cultural activities to do in the islands such as Traditional Tattoo, Hilot (A kind of traditonal healing),Pottery , and Habi (Traditional weaving). These activities helped the locals to gain income and enhance their business in order to attract more adventure seekers to a journey they will never forget.
